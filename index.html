Royal Mithila Food Palace is a multi-cuisine restaurant where we serve Indian, Nepali, Chinese, Italian, and Continental foods with a fusion of local taste.

Our Story

Inspired by the rich heritage of the Mithila region, we bring you a dining experience that is both royal and authentic. Our chefs specialize in blending traditional spices with modern culinary techniques.

Whether it's the crunch of a perfect Samosa or the delicate flavors of Italian pasta, every dish at Royal Mithila is prepared with the finest ingredients and a touch of Madhubani elegance.
